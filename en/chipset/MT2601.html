MT2601

Overview

MediaTek MT2601 offers dual-core performance to wearable devices without taking a toll on battery life. MT2601 comprises of 1.2 GHz dual-core ARM Cortex-A7 CPU, ARM Mali-400 MP GPU, ePOP memory link and a display controller that supports up to qHD resolution smoothly.


MediaTek MT2601 can interface with a range of external sensors and our MT6630 wireless connectivity chip to offer Wi-Fi, GNSS, Bluetooth and ANT+, all in a small PCBA footprint. It’s also compatible with our MT6323 power management and MT6605 NFC chips for additional hardware flexibility.


Meeting the design requirements of various wearable devices such as Android Wear-based sports and fitness devices, MT2601 enables location-tracking. Its multimedia subsystem fulfills entertainment needs thanks to camera support (up to 5MP) and the ability to play and record videos that are up to 720p.


With MediaTek MT2601, you can lower costs of other elements in the bill-of-materials including layout area and battery thanks to its leading integrated energy-efficient design.

Features

High-performance, low-energy

  • Designed for wearable devices, MT2601 features a Dual-Core 1.2GHz processor based on ARM Cortex-A7, which strikes the optimal balance between speed and power-efficiency. This CPU is complemented by Bluetooth Low Energy technology to further minimize battery drain. MediaTek MT2601’s multitasking capabilities will amaze you.

Integrated technologies

  • More than a processor, MT2601 is a highly integrated and robust system-on-chip featuring connectivity, satellite positioning (MT6630) and power management (MT6323) components that provides an effective all-in-one solution for small form-factor connected devices.

Multimedia optimized

  • MediaTek MT2601’s market-leading multimedia subsystem offers a comprehensive display and entertainment solution for a great reading experience.

Ecosystem-ready

  • Capable of encoding and decoding H.264 video, the MediaTek MT2601 is also ideal to run advanced Android Wear apps that’s packed with rich contents such as images and animations. MT2601 delivers a complete Android Wear experience, thanks to its superior integration of hardware and software.

Specifications

  • CPU: ARM Cortex-A7 at 1.2GHz

  • GPU: ARM Mali-400

  • Memory: eMMC + LPDDR2/3 (up to 4GB)

  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi 802.11abgn/ac1T1R, Bluetooth 3.0+HS, Bluetooth Low Energy 4.1, ANT+, GPS / GLONASS / Beidou / Galileo / QZSS (with MT6630)

  • Camera: up to 5MP primary camera and 1.3MP sub-camera

  • Display: up to qHD (960x540 resolution)

  • Integration: compatible with 3G modem, MT6605 NFC and MT6323 PMIC. Interfaces: sensors, cards and external sensor hub
